Countries with the Most Billionaires

Billionaire wealth is heavily concentrated in a few countries. According to Forbes’ 2025 list, the United States leads the world with 902 billionaires, followed by China (450) and India (205). Germany (171) and Russia (140) complete the top five. Canada (76), Italy (74) and Hong Kong (66) round out the top eight.
