Frequently Asked Questions

Is Billionaire Watch free to use?
Yes! Billionaire Watch is completely free to use with no hidden charges or subscription fees. We believe economic education should be accessible to everyone.
Do I need to create an account?
No registration or account creation is required. Simply visit our website and start exploring billionaire wealth data immediately.
Is my data safe?
Yes! We don't collect personal data from our users. All billionaire data displayed is from public sources, and we don't track your browsing activity.
How often is the data updated?
Our data is based on February 2026 estimates. We revise estimates quarterly or when major funding events occur.
Can I use this data for research?
Yes! Our data is suitable for educational and research purposes. Please credit Billionaire Watch when using our data in your work.
How do you estimate net worth?
We value public company holdings using recent share prices and disclosed ownership. For private companies, we use funding rounds, revenue multiples, and comparable public‑company valuations. Tangible assets (real estate, art) are included where reliable estimates exist, and known debts are subtracted. All figures are for educational purposes and not financial advice.
Where do you source your data?
We compile information from publicly available sources such as Forbes, Bloomberg, SEC filings and reputable news reports. Our goal is to provide transparent educational data without collecting any personal user information.
