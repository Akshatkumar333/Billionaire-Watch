Frequently Asked Questions About Mark Zuckerberg

How much of Meta does Mark Zuckerberg own today?

As of February 2026, Mark Zuckerberg owns approximately 13% of Meta's outstanding shares, representing about 350 million shares. However, due to dual-class share structure, he maintains majority voting control (~58% of voting power).

What is Mark Zuckerberg's salary?

Zuckerberg takes a symbolic $1 annual salary from Meta. His compensation comes entirely from stock appreciation. He has declined bonuses and equity grants since 2013.

What is the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative?

The Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI) is a philanthropic organization founded by Mark Zuckerberg and Priscilla Chan. It focuses on science, education, and justice issues, with a commitment to donate 99% of their Meta shares over their lifetimes.

How is Meta's metaverse bet performing?

By 2026, Reality Labs (metaverse division) has become profitable, generating $45 billion in annual revenue from hardware sales, virtual goods, and enterprise solutions. Horizon Worlds has 150 million monthly active users.

What are Zuckerberg's major investments outside Meta?

Zuckerberg has invested in AI research, biotechnology (through CZI), and real estate. Notable investments include the Biohub for scientific research and significant land holdings in Hawaii for conservation.
