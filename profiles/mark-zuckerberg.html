Frequently Asked Questions About Mark Zuckerberg

How much of Meta does Mark Zuckerberg own?

As of January 2026, Zuckerberg owns approximately 13% of Meta's outstanding shares, giving him significant control through special voting shares. This ownership represents about 86% of his total net worth.

What is Mark Zuckerberg's salary at Meta?

Zuckerberg famously takes a $1 annual salary. His compensation comes entirely from stock appreciation. The company spends approximately $14 million annually on his personal security.

How much has Meta invested in the metaverse?

Meta has invested over $50 billion in Reality Labs since 2020. The division became profitable in 2025, generating $2.1 billion in profit on $8.5 billion in revenue from VR hardware and software sales.

What is the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative?

The Chan Zuckerberg Initiative is a philanthropic organization founded by Mark Zuckerberg and Priscilla Chan. It has committed $45 billion to advance human potential and promote equality in areas such as science, education, and justice reform.

How has Meta's AI strategy evolved?

Meta has shifted from primarily using AI for content recommendations to developing general AI assistants, open-source AI models, and AI-powered creative tools. The company released Llama 3 in 2024 and is developing more advanced models for 2026 release.
