Frequently Asked Questions About Larry Page

How much of Alphabet does Larry Page own?

As of February 2026, Larry Page owns approximately 6% of Alphabet's outstanding shares, representing about 85 million Class A and Class C shares. This ownership accounts for approximately 85% of his total net worth.

What is Larry Page's role at Alphabet today?

Although he stepped down as CEO in 2019, Page remains a board member and major shareholder. He returned to an active advisory role in 2023 to oversee Alphabet's AI development strategy.

How does Larry Page's wealth compare to Sergey Brin's?

In 2026, Larry Page's net worth is $256.9B compared to Sergey Brin's $237.1B. The $19.8B difference comes from Page's slightly larger initial Google ownership and different investment portfolios.

What are Larry Page's major investments outside Alphabet?

Page has invested heavily in flying car companies (Kitty Hawk, Zee.Aero), renewable energy (Bloom Energy), and biotechnology through his venture capital funds.
