Biography

Mukesh Ambani remains India's richest person with approximately $92.5 billion in February 2026. As Chairman and Managing Director of Reliance Industries Limited, he leads one of India's largest conglomerates spanning petrochemicals, telecommunications, retail, and renewable energy. His strategic vision transformed Reliance from a traditional oil and petrochemicals company into a technology and consumer-focused empire.
